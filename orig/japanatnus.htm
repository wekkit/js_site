Welcome to the Japan@NUS website.  Japan is well represented at NUS, not only by researchers who are based in the Department of Japanese Studies, but also by researchers from other faculties and departments with a connection to Japan.  This site lists academics who study and teach about Japan as well as colleagues who are from Japan or have studied in Japan.  With this site, we want to provide points of contact for researchers outside NUS with an interest in Japan, and we hope for increased cooperation between NUS colleagues interested in Japan.  Please also note the below links to Japan related student activites:

For NUS colleagues: If we have overlooked you or if you are new at NUS and want to be featured here, please contact us at japanatnus@nus.edu.sg.
