Congratulations to Jennifer Ding for Achieving Jun-shihan Certification in Enshu Sado


Congratulations to Jennifer Ding, alumni of NUS Sado Club, who has achieved the honor of Jun-shihan certification in Enshu Sado!  This certification is a recognition to Jennifer's mastery of the art of Sado under the Enshu school, and with this certification, she is now qualified as an instructor of Sado.

Jennifer, who graduated from NUS in 2005, has joined the NUS Sado Club since she was a student in 2002.  Currently an employee with Panasonic Electronic Devices Pte Ltd., she continued the study of Sado under Hirai sensei in Osaka when she was re-locatied to Japan by the company in 2010.

Jennifer has continued to return to the club on the weekends for practice with fellow students and alumni since her return from Japan.  She is the second Singaporean to have received the honor of Jun-shihan, the first, also our alumni, is June Lim who was awarded in early 1990s.
