Workshop on Japanese Culture in Southeast Asia
(funded by The Japan Foundation)

Date: 13 – 14 October 2011
Venue: AS4-03-28 (Meeting Room)
Department of Japanese Studies, National University of Singapore
Free Admission (Registration required)

The Department of Japanese Studies at the National University of Singapore is organizing a workshop on Japanese Culture in Southeast Asia on 13 and 14 October 2011.  The two-day workshop seeks to assess the current state of research in this area, with a special focus on contemporary and popular culture. Participants at the workshop will address issues of production, transmission, representations, adaptation, reception as well as the politics of Japanese culture in Southeast Asia.  The workshop also aims to explore the potential for future projects, especially collaborative, comparative and cross-cultural research to expand our understanding on the development of Japanese culture in Soutehast Asia.
