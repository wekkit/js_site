JS Alumni won Japanese Speech Contest 2011 (Open Category)


Congratulations to Ms Chia Miaw Ling, Elly, JS Alumni of 1990, who won the Japanese Speech Contest 2011 (Open Category) held at the Japanese Association's auditorium on 17 July (Sunday). 

The annual contest, jointly organized by the Embassy of Japan, the Japanese Cultural Society Singapore and others, showcased a wide range of brilliant speeches by Japanese language learners who advanced through the preliminary and semi-final rounds. 

Elly took part for the first time in this Contest.  Drawing on her work experience, she overwhelmed the judges and a packed auditorium with her fluent delivery of an original speech on pointers for working happily in a Japanese company (「日本人と一緒に楽しく仕事をするために」).  She took home the highest honor for the day, the Japanese Ambassador's Cup, and a homestay programme at the Shizuoka prefecture. 

Elly is currently working in Denka Singapore Pte Ltd as its General Affairs Manager.  She continues to learn the Japanese language in her daily work.  We are proud of you, Elly!
