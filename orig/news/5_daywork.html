Implementation of the 5-day Workweek

The Department of Japanese Studies is implementing a 5-day workweek with effect from 1 October 2004.

This is in support of the national policy to adopt more pro-family employment practices and promote a healthy work life balance.

The new working hours will be:

Monday – Thursday: 8.30 am – 6.00 pm

Friday : 8.30 am – 5.30 pm

(Closed on Saturday, Sunday and Public Holidays)

We look forward to be of service to you with the extended hours on weekdays.


Department of Japanese Studies
Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences
30 September 2004
