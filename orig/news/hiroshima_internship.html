Hiroshima Singapore Association Internship Programme 2005

Period of homestay: 18 May to 10 June 2005 (two-day language training + three-week internship)

Number of students to be accepted: 9

Sponsors:
Hiroshima Shinyo Bank (2)
Japan Travel Bureau (1)
Rihga Royal Hotel Hiroshima (1)
Molten (manufacturing gum products (2)
Kinki Nihon Tourist (travel agency) (1)
Hiroshima ANA Hotel (2)
Sponsors will provide accommodations, either with homestay or company dormitories.

Estimated expenses: S$2,800
This includes:
1) round-trip airfare and transportation in Japan
2) training fee at the tranining center (¥19,000)
3) all meals and daily expenses
4) overseas travel insurance (mandatory)
Hiroshima Singapore Association will provide each participant a sum of ¥20,000.

Selection criteria:
1) All NUS students, but preference will be given to JS major students.
2) Participants must have basic communication ability in Japanese.
3) Everything being equal, early applications will be given priority.

Deadline for application: 1:00pm on Monday, 25 April 2005

Application form: Available at any IVLE site (workbin) of your JS/CLS modules

Application notification: Names of successful applicants will be posted on the Department of Japanese Studies homepage http://www.fas.nus.edu.sg/jps/ by Tuesday, 26 April.

Enquiry and form submission: Ms Lee Bee Ling (jpsleebl@nus.edu.sg) at the JS General Office (AS4/03-27).

Orientation session: Selected participants will have to attend a briefing session before departure. Date and time to be announced at a later date.
